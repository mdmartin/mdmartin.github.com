Me

I am a senior student in Peking University, China. My major is Computer Science. I am a member of Multimedia Information Processing Lab, Institute of Computer and Technology, Peking University. I'm interested in Information Retrieval, Cross-Media Retrieval, Visualization, and Machine Learning. By applying theories of those areas to interesting and practical programs, I find those theories really make differences. I realize what they can do about helping the information world develop, during which I also find me enjoying processing text, images, etc. I've made up my mind to seek for further study about Information Retrieval and related areas.
